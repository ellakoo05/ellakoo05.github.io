Technical Drawing






Role: Designer
Client: BCIT Term 2
Timeline: February 2018
Tools: Adobe Illustrator



Description

This was one of the assignments from my Advanced Photoshop class in term 3. I was assigned to select a real photograph and digitally paint it with Adobe Photoshop. I chose one of my favourite actresses, Anne Hathaway, and found her magazine cover photo. This assignment was very time consuming but I but I had fun time making the overall product and I am quite happy with the result.

Process

When I was assigned with this project, I had so many things going on my mind. I was awed with other students' results. I started early enough so I could put my time into this project. Firstly, I had to start with the base painting of Anne Hathaway. Thankfully, she did not have much hair. However, her jacket was a lot harder to draw than I thought.
