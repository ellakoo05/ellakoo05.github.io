Role: Designer
Client: BCIT Term 3
Timeline: October 2018
Tools: Adobe InDesign
Link: E-Magazine


Description

During Summer, all the students who are going into second year had to prepare a pitch presentation with their mobile application ideas. I thought of an idea where the users can directly pre-purchase gas from mobile application. Luckily, FuelHub got voted for top ten and I could proceed with this idea. During the term, I picked three other team members to work on this project â€“ the result was very successful and FuelHub was voted the first place for the final presentation.

Process

My idea was to let users buy gas with a mobile application. First, I had to fetch all the gas stations information and gas prices near the mobile device. I scraped off gas station names, current prices, and the location through GasBuddy's html data. I used Firebase for the back-end and stored all the user's data and used it throughout the application. It was challenging to code with React and Redux for the first time, but it was rewarding at the end.
